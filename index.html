Frequently Asked Questions

What makes GoodBoop different from big apps?

GoodBoop is locally owned and operated right here in your community. You’ll meet your sitter before services begin, and your pets will be cared for by a familiar face every time.

Are you insured?

Yes. We carry insurance through Pet Care Insurance to make sure our clients are protected.

How do I book?

All new clients begin with a short meet & greet scheduled through the Time To Pet app. A $20 deposit is required, credited toward your first booking. Click the "New Client" button to get started.

Which areas do you serve?

Concord Township, Mentor, Chardon, and Painesville. We focus our routes to stay punctual and maximize time with your pup.

Do you offer weekend/holiday care?

Yes—weekend visits are available! GoodBoop is closed on major holidays including Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, and Christmas Day so our team can spend time with family!

Do you stay overnight?

We offer Almost Overnight Care: extended evening and morning visits for pets who are comfortable sleeping alone, giving peace of mind without a full live-in stay.

Do you only house sit for dogs?

Nope! We love all pets — from cats and fish to most small household companions. If they’re part of your family, they’re part of ours.
